## 🚀 Overview
SpeedTree for Windows is a dedicated toolset for authoring 3D vegetation, from single hero trees to sprawling forests of plants and undergrowth. It combines procedural generation with hand-guided control so you can shape trunks, branches, leaves, and wind behavior, then render or export the result for a wide range of projects. Studios reach for SpeedTree software when they need realistic, production-ready foliage for games, film sequences, and architectural or scientific visualization. Because assets travel cleanly into real-time engines, the tool fits naturally into modern game and film pipelines.

## ✨ Features
- **Procedural tree and plant generation** — Build trunks, branches, and foliage from parametric rules, then refine any part by hand for a tailored result.
- **Engine export and integration** — Send finished models to real-time engines, including SpeedTree Unity support and export to Unreal, so foliage looks consistent in-game.
- **Ready-made model library** — Start from the SpeedTree library of prebuilt trees and plants and adapt them to your scene instead of modeling every species from scratch.
- **Wind and seasonal animation** — Author natural wind motion and seasonal variation so vegetation reacts and changes across your shots.
- **Level-of-detail control** — Generate optimized LODs and billboards to keep dense forests performant in games and interactive visualization.

## 🎯 Getting Started
1. Open SpeedTree and create a new model or load a tree from the built-in library.
2. Use the procedural generators to shape the trunk, branches, and leaves, adjusting parameters until the plant looks right.
3. Add wind and seasonal settings to bring natural motion and variation to your vegetation.
4. Generate levels of detail so the model stays efficient in dense scenes.
5. Export the finished asset to your target engine, such as Unity or Unreal, and drop it into your project.
